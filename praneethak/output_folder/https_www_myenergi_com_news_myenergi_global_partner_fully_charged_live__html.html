myenergi announced as global innovation partner for Fully Charged LIVE!

Home - Blog - myenergi announced as global innovation partner for Fully Charged LIVE!

We’re delighted to announce that myenergi has teamed up with Fully Charged LIVE as global innovation partner for 2023.

The agreement will see us travel the world – from Australia and Canada, to America and Amsterdam – for each international instalment of the world’s leading electric vehicle and home energy show.

Since the very first Fully Charged LIVE show in 2018, we’ve been a regular exhibitor, travelling across Europe and North America to showcase our latest product innovations. With the show set to expand its world tour in 2023, we were keen to put pen to paper as the event’s first official partner.

The show schedule has already been confirmed, with the myenergi team already preparing for the first event in Australia next month:

• Fully Charged LIVE Australia – 11-12 March, ICC Sydney

• Fully Charged LIVE UK South – 28-30 April, Farnborough International

• Fully Charged LIVE UK North – 19-21 May, Yorkshire Event Centre

• Fully Charged LIVE Canada – 8-10 September, Vancouver Convention Centre

• Fully Charged LIVE USA – 27-29 October, Del Mar Fairgrounds, San Diego

• Fully Charged LIVE Europe – 24-26 November, RAI Amsterdam

Jordan Brompton, co-founder and CMO of myenergi, explains:

“As one of the world’s fastest-growing eco-smart tech companies, our products are used by homeowners all over the globe. To that end, it makes absolute sense to partner with Fully Charged LIVE on a global basis for all six events this year. We have always admired what the team is aiming to do, with a shared ambition to support the switch to renewable energy, electric vehicles and eco-smart homes.”

CEO of Fully Charged Live, Dan Caesar, adds:

“We have worked closely with myenergi over the past six years, and we are delighted to further strengthen our relationship with their confirmation as Fully Charged LIVE’s first ‘global partner’. Their commitment to support all six of our LIVE shows in 2023 is testament to the success of the events and also the innovative vision of their organisation.

“With plans to take its business into further global markets we know Fully Charged LIVE offers myenergi the perfect platform to reach thousands of new potential customers.”

For more information about Fully Charged LIVE, visit: https://fullycharged.live/.
