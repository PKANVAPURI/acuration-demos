alternatives mean that we are now seeing some of the best electric vehicles ever produced in terms of capability and range the level of innovation automakers are currently achieving and working list to discover the electric vehicles with the longest range currently available on the market 1 tesla model s long range  370 miles it comes as no surprise that elon musks tesla the brand that has arguably singlehandedly revived the electric car revolution sits at the top of our list the model s is the perfect embodiment of teslas commitment to innovation and technology boasting allwheeldrive a 17 touchscreen and an engine capable of 060mph in as little as 24 seconds auto express described the model s as an executive car for industry pioneers that want to make a statement giving it an impressive 45 stars out of 5 2 tesla model 3  329 miles tesla are well and truly smashing the ev market at the moment producing three of the models with the best range next up is the model 3 with a range of 329 miles the model 3 possesses some of elon musks most impressive safety features including a metal structure capable of resisting four times the vehicles own mass which is the equivalent of two fullgrown african elephants with preorders available for delivery from june 2019 excitement is high for the model 3 especially due to its slightly lower price of roughly 38900 3 tesla model x  315 miles last but certainly not least in our tesla entries is the model x the quickest most capable sport utility vehicle in history according to tesla seating up to seven adults and with stacks of storage space this is arguably teslas most practical model not to mention the fact it can travel from 060mph in an exhilarating 27 seconds while the model xs 325mile range is one of the best it did score lower than the model s when reviewed by auto express achieving 3 out of 5 stars 4 jaguar ipace  292 miles jaguars firstever allelectric vehicle has been a roaring success with the ipace recently collecting a recordbreaking three accolades at the world car awards the electric suv was the first car to win world car of the year world car design of the year and green car of the year at the 2019 ceremony one glance inside our very own ipace sitting in the myenergi car park and youll understand why its won so many awards and rave reviews its sleek futuristic interior is matched by elegant exterior design and a performance capable of 060mph in 45 seconds available from 64495 the ipace was rewarded with 5 stars from whatcar 5 kia eniro  282 miles whatcar electric car of the year and car of the year the eniro has certainly made quite the impression with motorists and critics since its unveiling earlier this year drivers of the eniro will benefit from a 7year warranty assuring maximum
