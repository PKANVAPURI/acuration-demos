distillers commented earlier this year we relocated to a site fourteen times the size of our previous distillery the new space is perfect for our rapid expansion plans and will house our brand new 5000 litre still while the new distillery is almost operational were also in the process of creating a visitor centre which is set to open later this year installing the zappi chargers will allow guests to charge their cars when they come to visit as well as looking great and providing a huge variety of charging modes theyre the most sustainable charging point on the market  a hugely important
