accolades the journey so far home  blog  myenergi awards  accolades the journey so far the renewables industry is one of the most rewarding and fulfilling sectors in which to operate striving together to create a more sustainable future alongside fellow ecowarriors gives us great joy and satisfaction to us creating a brighter future for the planet we call home is more than just a job it is a collective mission that we all share inside and outside of work what started in 2017 as a small green tech company in binbrook lincolnshire has grown to a fantastic team of 65 people shipping over 40000 products we have filed patents written books and released plenty of new and innovative green products the myenergi mission has always been  and always will be  to change the world through green technology and we are extremely proud of what we have achieved so far throughout this amazing journey our team has been rewarded and nominated for accolades and awards that span across the sector and the local area while we are fully aware that our adventure has only just begun and that there is plenty more work to be done to save our environment we know how important it is to celebrate our successes and look back on what we have achieved so far here are some of the awards currently sitting in the myenergi trophy cabinet myenergi awards  accolades green innovation award the humber
