the transition from petrol and diesel vehicles over to plugin electric vehicles electric vehicles dont release carbon and dangerous gasses into the atmosphere and therefore are much better for the environment governments worldwide are driving the notion towards a netzero carbon target in an attempt to slow the pace of global warming amongst many other major benefits these include increasing australias ev manufacturing and design sectors which in turn creates new skills and jobs and cleaner air in our towns and cities funding available for electric cars in australia there is limited funding available for electric cars however if you are looking to purchase an electric car in victoria or new south wales there is certainly more support for you we are expecting the electric car grants available to become more widely available as they are within other parts of the world but there is currently no set timescale on this alongside developments in new technology such as myenergis
