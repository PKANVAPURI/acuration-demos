where a battery storage system like libbi comes in handy by charging the battery during offpeak times you can then use this stored electricity during peak times to keep your costs down if you work capabilities of libbi by storing excess solar energy when your panels are generating more than you need libbi can store this meaning you wont need to rely on the grid during the evenings or
